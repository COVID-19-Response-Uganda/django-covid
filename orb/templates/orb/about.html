{% trans 'About ORB' %}

{% blocktrans %}

ORB offers frontline health workers and trainers access to quality assured open source content that can be used on mobile devices and shared virally amongst communities.

ORB has three unique features:

  1. Brings into one space quality-assured, multimedia materials from multiple content developers, with a focus on maternal and child health.
  2. Adaptation of existing content ORB aims to reduce the practice of new content being developed unnecessarily.
  3. A global collaborative network of organizations to share and review content, integrate content into programs and share user-experience.

By improving access to health content and mobile learning, ORB helps health workers access the vital content they need to do their work effectively and confidently.

ORB has been developed by the mPowering Frontline Health Workers partnership.

{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Content Review Team' %}

{% blocktrans %}

Our Content Review Team consists of staff from over 35 organsations, with backgrounds in global public health, training and technology.

When resources are submitted to ORB, the Content Review Team makes the initial decision on the suitability of the resource to be included in ORB. Once a resource has been approved by the Content Review Team it is then passed to our Medical Expert Panel.

{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Medical Expert Panel' %}

{% blocktrans %}

Our Medical Expert Panel consists of medical professionals from international health organisations.

When a submitted resource has been approved by Content Review Team, the Medical Expert Panel makes the final decision on whether a resource can be included in ORB, based on the most up to date medical information and guidelines available.

{% endblocktrans %}
