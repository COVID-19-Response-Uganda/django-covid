{% trans 'About ORB' %}

{% blocktrans %}

ORB offers frontline health workers and trainers access to quality assured open source content that can be used on mobile devices and shared virally amongst communities.

ORB brings into one space curated, quality- assured, multimedia materials from multiple content developers. The content covers the RMNCH spectrum for health workers and supervisors. ORB promotes open source content, and encourages re-use and adaptation of content to reduce the practice of trainers developing new content unnecessarily. It improves access to mHealth content and mobile learning so that training can reach significantly more health workers. Finally, ORB brings together a global collaborative network of organizations to share their content, review content and user experience, integrate content into programs, and advocate for use of the platform. Including WASH, nutrition, and violence

ORB has been developed by the mPowering Frontline Health Workers coalition.
